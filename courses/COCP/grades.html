Grades and Credits

Course 1DL451 and Part 1 of Course 1DL442: Modelling for Combinatorial Optimisation

The three modelling assignments are worth 3 higher-education credits (ECTS credits). The modelling assignment grade is as follows when \(a_i\) is your final score (out of 5, or pass / fail) on modelling assignment \(i \in 1..3\):

Grade Condition
5 \(a_1 = \text{pass} \land a_2+a_3 \in 9..10\)
4 \(a_1 = \text{pass} \land a_2+a_3 \in 7..8\)
3 \(a_1 = \text{pass} \land a_2+a_3 \in 5..6 \land a_2 \cdot a_3 > 0\)

The project is worth 2 higher-education credits (ECTS credits). The project grade is as follows when \(p\) is your project score (out of 10) and you earned a 'pass' grade on all the intermediate project deliverables:

Grade Condition
5 \(p \in 9..10\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture
4 \(p \in 7..8\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture
3 \(p \in 5..6\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture

A missed guest lecture can be compensated for (in case of no force majeure) by a summary of a research paper chosen by the head teacher.

The overall grade for Course 1DL451 is as follows when you have earned the 3 modelling assignment credits with total score \(m=a_2+a_3\) (out of 10) and the 2 project credits with score \(p\) (also out of 10), the weights being the numbers of credits:

Swedish Grade Condition
5 \(3m + 2p \in 43..50 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5\)
4 \(3m + 2p \in 33..42 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5\)
3 \(3m + 2p \in 25..32 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5\)

Part 2 of Course 1DL442: Constraint Programming

The three constraint-programming assignments are worth 5 higher-education credits (ECTS credits). The constraint-programming assignment grade is as follows when \(a_i\) is your final score (out of 5, or pass / fail) on constraint-programming assignment \(i \in 4..6\):

Grade Condition
5 \(a_4 = \text{pass} \land a_5+a_6 \in 9..10\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture
4 \(a_4 = \text{pass} \land a_5+a_6 \in 7..8\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture
3 \(a_4 = \text{pass} \land a_5+a_6 \in 5..6 \land a_5 \cdot a_6 > 0\) and attendance at mandatory guest lecture

A missed guest lecture can be compensated for (in case of no force majeure) by a summary of a research paper chosen by the head teacher.

The overall grade for Course 1DL442 is as follows when you have earned the 3 modelling assignment credits with total score \(m=a_2+a_3\) (out of 10) and the 2 project credits with score \(p\) (also out of 10) and the 5 constraint-programming assignment credits with total score \(c=a_5+a_6\) (again out of 10), the weights being the numbers of credits:

Swedish Grade Condition
5 \(3m + 2p + 5c \in 85..100 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5 \land c \geq 5\)
4 \(3m + 2p + 5c \in 65..84 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5 \land c \geq 5\)
3 \(3m + 2p + 5c \in 50..64 \land m \geq 5 \land p \geq 5 \land c \geq 5\)
