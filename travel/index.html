The Countries and Territories I Have Visited

I have spent at least one day in the following countries and territories, so I can pretend knowing a little bit about them (the world's nicest places, in my humble opinion, are spelled in pink):

  • in Europe:
    • Luxembourg: my beloved home-country, in which I have seen literally every corner
    • Belgium: I studied in Liège, Namur, and Louvain-la-Neuve, and have been all over the country: Arlon, Bouillon, Durbuy, Orval, Spa, Verviers, Brussels, Bruges, Oostende, Koksijde, Wenduine, Blankenberge, ...
    • The Netherlands: Amsterdam, Delft, Eindhoven, Maastricht, ...
    • France: I have lived for 5 months in Grenoble, and visited Paris, Lille, Toulouse, Marseille, Reims, Metz, Dijon, Lyon, Nantes, Montpellier, Nice, Antibes, ...
    • Germany: Aachen, Trier, Saarburg, München, Darmstadt, Freiburg, Marktoberdorf, Berlin, Mönchengladbach, ...
    • Switzerland: Zürich, Kandersteg, Interlaken, Geneva, ...
    • Austria: Vienna, Salzburg
    • Liechtenstein: Vaduz
    • United Kingdom: London, Manchester, Bristol, Edinburgh, Inverness, Aberdeen, St Andrews, ...
    • Sweden: I live in Uppsala since 1998, and visited Stockholm, Lund, Malmö, Ystad, Göteborg, Sundsvall, Umeå, Kiruna, Örebro, ...
